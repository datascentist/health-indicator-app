How it works

To use this health tool, first you will choose heart disease or diabete disease prediction, hit on heart or diabete button, then fill the out the form and click check button and wait while app is predicting, after it will show you the result (Positive or Negative) and life style recommendations based on your heath data you have enterd through the form.
