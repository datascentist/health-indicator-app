The following form takes your health related data and check if you have heart issues, its not 100% real, if you have health potential issues you're advised to visit your medical services center.

Heart Disease Prediction

Personal Information

Enter your Age Choose Your Gender

Vital Signs

Enter your resting blood pressure in mm Hg. Normal is typically below 120. Enter the maximum heart rate you achieved during exercise.

Medical History

Select "Yes" if your fasting blood sugar is greater than 120 mg/dL. Select "Yes" if you experience angina (chest pain) during exercise.

Laboratory Results

Enter your cholesterol level in mg/dL. A normal reading is typically below 200.

Diagnostic Tests

Enter the ST depression value. This measures the difference in the ST segment during exercise and rest. Select the shape of your ST segment during peak exercise.
